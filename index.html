1. Assign instructional coach to work with new teachers on instructional methods.
2. Display student work prominently in school common areas and classrooms.
3. Employ floating substitute to enable teachers to team teach on occasion.
4. Encourage grade levels teams create a two week interdisciplinary project related to state standads.
5. Establish a target that a minimum 50 percent of class time be student activity, not teacher talk.
6. Establish requirement that each teacher have “bell ringer “ activity for students to work on as class begins.
7. Follow district policy that learning objectives for each class be posted on the board in each classroom.
8. Have administrative staff do 50 classroom walkthroughs per week.
9. Offer school wide teacher workshop on differentiated Instruction.
10. Put to a faculty vote on whether to use 90 minute or 45 minute classes.
11. Require teachers to assign homework each night.
12. Require that all teachers write one Gold Seal Lesson related to 21st Century skill.
13. Require that weekly lessons plans be submitted Principal’s office.
14. Set up staff video resource with samples of various instructional strategies.
15. Use instructional coaches to schedule exemplary lessons that other teachers can observe.
16. Work with student leadership to create monthly teacher award for outstanding Quadrant D lessons.
